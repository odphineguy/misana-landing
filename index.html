What makes MiSana different

Built for trust, clarity, and everyday family health.

MiSana is designed for real life: organizing symptom notes for doctor visits, looking up medication information for abuelitos and kids, and walking into appointments better prepared.

✓

100% private by design

MiSana runs an AI model directly on your iPhone. Your health data never leaves your device — no servers, no cloud processing, and no account required.

◔

Works offline anywhere

No signal? No problem. Everything runs locally on your phone, so you can look up medications, review health notes, and access general health information even without internet access.

ES

Truly bilingual

Full support for Spanish and English, including Mexican Spanish and Spanglish. Ask questions in the language your family actually uses at home.

♥

Reads Apple Health

With your permission, MiSana reads your steps, heart rate, sleep, blood pressure, blood oxygen, and more from Apple Health (read-only — we never modify your data).

Features

Everything your family needs for everyday health questions.

MiSana brings together health information, medication lookup, symptom note organization, and appointment prep in one place — so you can feel more informed before, during, and after care.

1

Home

See your health snapshot powered by Apple Health data, including steps, heart rate, sleep quality, and trends.

2

Medications

Scan medication barcodes with your camera or search by name. Keep track of your family's medications in one place.

3

Symptoms

Organize your symptom notes and find general educational information from cited sources to help prepare for conversations with your doctor.

4

Appointments

Prepare suggested questions to discuss with your healthcare provider based on your health notes and concerns.

5

Ask

Chat with MiSana's on-device AI about general health topics in Spanish or English.
