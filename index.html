What makes MiSana different

Your family's health story, organized and private.

MiSana isn't a symptom checker or an AI doctor. It's a notebook that helps you capture what you feel, see what keeps coming back, and show your doctor the full picture.

✓

100% private by design

MiSana keeps everything on your iPhone. Your symptoms, patterns, and health notes never leave your device — no servers, no cloud processing, no account required.

◔

Works offline anywhere

No signal? No problem. Log symptoms, review your patterns, and prepare for visits without an internet connection. Everything runs on your phone.

ES

Truly bilingual

Full support for Spanish and English, including Mexican Spanish and Spanglish. Use the language your family actually speaks at home.

♥

Reads Apple Health

With your permission, MiSana reads your steps, heart rate, sleep, blood pressure, blood oxygen, and more from Apple Health (read-only — we never modify your data).

How it works

From "I don't feel right" to "here's what my doctor needs to see."

MiSana turns scattered health notes into a clear story you can bring to your next appointment — so nothing gets lost and nothing gets forgotten.

1

Log

Capture symptoms with structured entries: what you felt, when, how bad, what helped. No blank pages — MiSana guides you through it.

2

Patterns

MiSana looks across your entries and surfaces what keeps showing up — recurring symptoms, timing, triggers. Your data, not a diagnosis.

3

Doctor Brief

Generate a one-page summary with your symptom timeline, patterns, current medications, and questions — ready to hand to your doctor or read from your phone.

4

Medications

Keep your family's medication list in one place. Search by name or scan a barcode to add.

5

Apple Health

See your health snapshot — steps, heart rate, sleep, and trends — pulled from Apple Health with your permission.

MiSana is NOT a healthcare provider, medical device, diagnostic tool, treatment tool, medication safety tool, or emergency service. MiSana is a personal health journal and organization tool. It helps you log symptoms, identify patterns in your own data, and organize information for doctor visits. It does not diagnose conditions, prescribe treatment, or replace professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making medical decisions.
